# Daily Suggestion card: dismiss and show another

The Daily Suggestion card on the AI Dev Command Center (`/admin/dev-command`) now gives you control over which suggestion you see.

## Dismiss (X)
Click the **X** next to a suggestion to hide it for good. It won't come back, even if you reload the page or log in again later. Dismissing is per-admin — other admins will still see it until they dismiss it themselves.

## Show another
Click **Show another** if you want to see a different suggestion right now without permanently dismissing the current one. It moves to the next suggestion in the queue. It won't repeat a suggestion you've already dismissed or already turned into a task.

## Turn into a task
When you turn a suggestion into a task, it's automatically linked to that task. Once that happens, the suggestion won't show again for any admin — no need to dismiss it separately.

## If there's nothing left
If you've dismissed or actioned every available suggestion, the card shows a friendly message letting you know there's nothing left for now — check back later for new suggestions.
